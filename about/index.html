JM Zhou
JM Zhou is now a Master student in ShanghaiTech University. His research interest is radio frequency (RF) devices and circuits. To date, he has publicated 6 works which are indexed by SCI/EI, and with 1 work being under reveiw. He was awarded a China National Scholarship in 2023. During 2022 to 2023 semester, he was awarded one of the outstanding students, as well as one of the outstanding teaching assistants. He is also a laboratory student assistant engineer in Post More Integrated Circuit Center (PMICC)
